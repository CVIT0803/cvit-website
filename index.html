About CVIT

Your Local IT Guy.
For Real.

Cole Verrell is the owner and operator of CVIT, LLC — a Tupelo-based IT support company built on the idea that small businesses deserve fast, reliable, local tech help without big-company prices or out-of-town response times.

With a background in data analysis and hands-on IT support, Cole brings a unique combination of technical depth and practical problem-solving to every client relationship. Whether it's a down printer on a busy Monday morning or a full office network setup, CVIT gets it handled.

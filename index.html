About CVIT

Your Local IT Guy. For Real.

Cole Verrell is the owner and operator of CVIT, LLC — a Tupelo-based IT support company built on the idea that small businesses deserve fast, reliable, local tech help without big-company prices or out-of-town response times.

With a background in data analysis and hands-on IT support, Cole brings a unique combination of technical depth and practical problem-solving to every client relationship.

CVIT operates on a simple principle: pick up the phone, show up, and fix it. No outsourced help desks. No ticketing systems. Just a local person who knows your setup and gets there fast.
